About Me

I am an experienced educator located in Dallas, Texas. With a strong foundation in teaching, I have been fortunate to have my mother as a dedicated mentor throughout my career. Her passion for education and commitment to student success greatly influenced my professional practice.

Driven by the creative aspects of instructional design, I enjoy crafting e-learning solutions that are not only impactful, but also engaging and effective. I am constantly seeking out new ideas and current emerging e-learning technologies to enhance my learning content.

Outside of instructional design, I pursue various hobbies that fuel my creativity. Whether it's delving into a captivating book, expressing myself through drawing, or exploring new destinations, I find inspiration in the world around me. Additionally, my proficiency in Adobe software and traditional media allows me to channel my artistic abilities into personal projects, including illustrations and other creative endeavors.

As an instructional designer, I am passionate about leveraging my expertise and skills to create meaningful learning experiences that empower learners and drive positive outcomes. I am excited to bring my dedication, creativity, and commitment to excellence to a dynamic team of professionals focused on educational innovation.
