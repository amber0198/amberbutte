About Me

I am an educator located in Dallas, Texas. For over 20 years, my mother dedicated herself to education and mentored me in the beginning of my teaching career. Her enthusiasm for learning and student success were goals that I carried towards my professional practice.

The creative process is one of my favorite aspects of instructional design. I strive to create e-learning solutions that are meaningful, engaging, and effective. I love to incorporate new ideas and emerging e-learning technologies in my design process.

In my free time I enjoy reading, traveling, and drawing with digital or traditional media.
